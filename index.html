★ Festivals & Joy

India is a land of perennial celebration. Timing your visit to coincide with a major festival offers a glimpse into the country's vibrant, collective spirit unlike any other experience.

Diwali: Festival of Lights

October/November. See Amritsar's Golden Temple lit up like a jewel box.

Holi: Festival of Colors

March. Participate in the playful chaos of colors in Mathura or Vrindavan.
