About Ladakh

Ladakh, located in the northernmost region of India, is a mystical land of stark mountains, high-altitude deserts, and breathtaking monasteries. Known as the "Land of High Passes", it offers an enchanting mix of Tibetan culture, rugged landscapes, and surreal lakes like Pangong and Tso Moriri. Adventure seekers find paradise here, with trekking, river rafting, and motorbike expeditions across some of the world's highest roads.

Plan Your Trip

📅 Best Time to Visit

May to September (roads open, pleasant weather, adventure season).

💰 Entry Info

No entry fee, but Inner Line Permits required for certain areas.

🕘 Travel Duration

Minimum 6–10 days recommended to explore Ladakh comfortably.

🌄 Travel Tips

Acclimatize for altitude, carry warm clothes, and stay hydrated.
