About the Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal, this ivory-white marble mausoleum is the finest example of Mughal architecture, combining Islamic, Persian, and Indian styles. Its symmetry, gardens, and aura make it one of the most visited monuments worldwide.
