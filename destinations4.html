About Varanasi

Varanasi, also known as Kashi or Benares, is one of the oldest living cities in the world. Located in Uttar Pradesh along the holy river Ganges, it is considered the spiritual capital of India. Famous for its Ghats, temples, and mesmerizing Ganga Aarti, Varanasi is where pilgrims come to wash away sins, seek moksha, and embrace eternal peace. Its vibrant culture, music, and ancient traditions make it a truly timeless destination.
